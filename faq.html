Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about our coffee, services, and locations.

General Questions

What makes Nuansa Bee coffee special?

At Nuansa Bee, we source only the highest quality beans from sustainable farms around the world. Our coffee is roasted in small batches to ensure optimal flavor and freshness. We also take pride in our unique brewing methods and signature blends that you won't find anywhere else.

What are your operating hours?

Our standard hours are:

  • Monday - Friday: 7:00 AM - 8:00 PM
  • Saturday: 8:00 AM - 9:00 PM
  • Sunday: 8:00 AM - 7:00 PM

Hours may vary by location. Please check our Locations page for specific store hours.

Do you offer Wi-Fi at your locations?

Yes, all Nuansa Bee locations offer complimentary high-speed Wi-Fi for our customers. Simply connect to the "NuansaBee-Guest" network and accept the terms of service to begin browsing.

Products & Menu

Do you offer non-dairy milk alternatives?

Yes, we offer a variety of non-dairy alternatives including almond milk, oat milk, soy milk, and coconut milk. There is a small additional charge for milk substitutions.

Do you have seasonal menu items?

Yes, we introduce new seasonal beverages and food items throughout the year. Our seasonal menu typically changes quarterly to showcase the best flavors of each season. Follow us on social media or sign up for our newsletter to stay updated on our latest offerings.

Can I purchase your coffee beans to brew at home?

Absolutely! We sell our signature blends and single-origin beans in all of our locations. You can purchase them whole or have them ground to your preferred brewing method. We also offer a subscription service for home delivery.

Ordering & Payment

Do you offer online ordering?

Yes, you can place orders through our mobile app or website for pickup at your preferred location. We also partner with several delivery services for home or office delivery in select areas.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, mobile payments (Apple Pay, Google Pay), and cash. You can also load money onto your Nuansa Bee rewards account for quick and easy payments.

Membership & Rewards

How does your loyalty program work?

Our Bee Rewards program lets you earn 1 point for every Rp10.000 spent. Once you accumulate 100 points, you'll receive a free drink of your choice. Members also get exclusive offers, birthday rewards, and early access to seasonal items.

Is there a fee to join your rewards program?

No, joining our Bee Rewards program is completely free. You can sign up through our mobile app, website, or in-store at any Nuansa Bee location.

Still Have Questions?

If you couldn't find the answer to your question, please feel free to contact us directly.
